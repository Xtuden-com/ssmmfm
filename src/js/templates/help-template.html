Content Rules

  • Authorized form should be in lowercase except proper nouns.
  • Use "with" to combine multiple components of dishes instead of punctuation. For more than one "with" item, we follow standard grammatical rules for lists (including Oxford comma).
  • Units of measures should come after the end of the dish and after a comma. Number should be spelled out. This includes halves, portions, etc.
  • We prefer natural language order.
  • We prefer modern spellings over archaic spellings.
  • We add diacritics if they are missing.
  • We remove lists of ingredients.
  • Drop any included prices.
  • Remove punctuation other than commas.
  • Expand abbreviations.
  • Place name and then preparation.
